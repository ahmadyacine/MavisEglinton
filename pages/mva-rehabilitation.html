Motor Vehicle Accident Rehabilitation in Mississauga

Recovering after a motor vehicle accident can involve physical, emotional, and administrative challenges. Our team offers multidisciplinary rehabilitation services and can help patients understand the general steps involved in accessing treatment.

Our MVA Rehabilitation Services

We provide a coordinated, multidisciplinary approach to support your recovery after an auto accident.

Accident Benefit Assistance

We assist with understanding the documentation and administrative process for accessing treatment coverage.

Physiotherapy

Targeted therapies to restore mobility, strength, and function after impact injuries.

Massage Therapy

Techniques to manage muscular tension, stress, and promote relaxation during recovery.

Chiropractic Care

Assessment and management of spinal, neck, and joint discomfort commonly associated with accidents.

Psychological Services

Support for the emotional and mental well-being challenges that can follow a traumatic event.

Rehabilitation Programs

Structured, progressive exercise and conditioning to help you return to daily activities safely.

The General Process

1

Contact the clinic

Reach out to us to book an initial assessment.

2

Provide basic accident and insurance information

This helps us understand your situation and coverage potential.

3

Attend an initial assessment

Meet with the appropriate practitioner to evaluate your injuries and concerns.

4

Receive a proposed treatment plan

Based on the assessment, a tailored approach to recovery will be suggested.

5

Complete required forms

Fill out any necessary documentation for your insurer, where applicable.

6

Begin approved treatment

Start your rehabilitation sessions as outlined in your plan.

7

Progress is reviewed

Your recovery is monitored throughout rehabilitation, and adjustments are made as needed.

Disclaimer: Insurance coverage and treatment authorization depend on the patient’s policy, accident-benefit eligibility, clinical assessment, insurer requirements, and applicable Ontario processes. The clinic cannot guarantee approval or coverage.

What to Bring to Your First Visit

  • Medication list
  • Referral documents, when applicable
