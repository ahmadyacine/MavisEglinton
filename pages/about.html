About Mavis Eglinton Physiotherapy

A multidisciplinary clinic dedicated to providing individualized, coordinated healthcare services for the Mississauga community.

Our Approach to Patient Care

At Mavis Eglinton Physiotherapy, we believe in a patient-centered approach. Our goal is to support your recovery, manage pain, and improve your mobility through comprehensive and structured care plans.

  • Listen to the patient’s concerns and health goals.
  • Perform an appropriate and thorough assessment.
  • Explain the proposed treatment approach clearly.
  • Develop an individualized care plan.
  • Monitor progress and adjust treatment when appropriate.
  • Coordinate services when multidisciplinary support is useful.

Multidisciplinary Services

Because recovery often benefits from multiple perspectives, we offer a range of services under one roof. Our practitioners can coordinate your care across physiotherapy, massage therapy, chiropractic care, and more to ensure a cohesive treatment plan.
