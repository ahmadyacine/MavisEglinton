Privacy Policy

How we handle your information and appointment requests.

Disclaimer: This privacy notice is provided as a general website template and should be reviewed by the clinic and an appropriately qualified Canadian privacy professional before publication.

At Mavis Eglinton Physiotherapy, we are committed to maintaining the privacy and security of the personal information you provide to us through this website.

What Information We Collect

When you use our online appointment request form or contact us via email, we collect the following information:

  • Full Name
  • Phone Number
  • Email Address
  • Requested Services
  • Preferred Appointment Date and Time
  • General notes or comments regarding scheduling

Why We Collect This Information

We collect this information strictly for the purpose of:

  • Reviewing and processing your appointment request.
  • Contacting you to confirm, modify, or schedule your appointment.
  • Responding to any questions or inquiries you have submitted.

How Appointment Information is Used

The information submitted via our online form is securely transmitted and reviewed by our clinic staff. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your information is only accessed by authorized clinic personnel responsible for scheduling and patient communication.

Third-Party Processing Services

Please note that appointment requests submitted through this website may be processed using configured third-party secure form handlers (such as Google Forms or Google Apps Script). While these services employ robust security measures, we advise against submitting highly sensitive medical data through the form.

Important Notice: Users should not submit emergency, crisis, or highly sensitive medical information through our contact form or email. If you are experiencing a medical emergency, please call 911 immediately.
